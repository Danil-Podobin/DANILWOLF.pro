I am a developer, I was born and live in Russia.

In 2015, I started my career as a video game developer, and I usually worked on GameMaker, Clickteam and Unity 3D engines. At about the same time, I decided to try myself in 3D modeling. After some time, I put aside all the developments and projects, and then completely abandoned them.

A year later, I seriously began to study JS, HTML, CSS, which further pushed me to create extensions for the Opera browser.

In the fall of 2017, I joined the VKontakte beta testing program. I liked this kind of activity, so I’m still testing VKontakte to this day.
